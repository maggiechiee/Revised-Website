ORIGINAL WORKS · HANDCRAFTED OBJECTS · LIMITED EDITIONS

From
feeling
to form.

Every work begins not with a canvas but with an interior state — something half-seen, half-remembered. The haze of golden afternoons. The weight of silence between two people. The particular green of rain on old stone.

Trained in the visual language of light and shadow, my work spans painting, lens-based photography, handcrafted sculpture and digital composition. Each medium chosen for what it can hold.
